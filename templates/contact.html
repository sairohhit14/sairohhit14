Frequently Asked Questions

You can book a consultation by visiting our Booking page. There, you can select a doctor, choose a preferred date and time, and specify whether you want an online or in-person consultation.

You can order over-the-counter medications without a prescription. For prescription medications, you must upload a valid prescription from a licensed healthcare provider. Visit our Medicine page for more information.

Our report scanning feature allows you to upload your medical reports in various formats. The system stores them securely, making them accessible when needed for consultations. You can upload your reports through the Report Scanning page.

For medical emergencies, call 911 immediately. Our Emergency page provides guidance on handling various emergency situations and lists nearby emergency facilities.

Our AI health assistant is available through the chat icon at the bottom right of the screen. You can type questions or use voice input to get assistance with common health queries and navigate our services.
